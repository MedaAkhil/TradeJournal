VWAP – The Fair Value Line

VWAP acts as the "fair value" line. When the price breaks the ORB High but is below VWAP, institutions and algo traders may sell into that rally — making the breakout unreliable.

When price is above VWAP and breaks out, the big money is on your side.

Pro Tip: Always check VWAP alignment before taking an opening range breakout. Price above VWAP + ORB Breakout = High probability trade.

What to Avoid

  • False breakouts — Price pokes above ORB High but closes back inside the range. Wait for a candle close above the level, not just a wick.
  • Trading during ORB window — Do not enter between 9:15–9:20 AM; let the range form first.
  • Narrow ORB ranges on flat days — A very small range (< 30–40 points on Nifty) means low conviction; signals from such days are often noise.
  • Trading against VWAP — If price breaks down below ORB Low but is above VWAP, skip the short trade.
